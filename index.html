About & links

A read-only engineering portfolio and system-navigation surface. It renders runbooks and lists build artifacts from OpenTelemetry performance, PostgreSQL RDS migration, and IAM security work, and maps how those connect to Odisena's domains, repositories, deployment surfaces, publication systems, and execution gates.

Odisena.World / Helios is preview-built (WebXR MVP preview-deployed). Its production domain and physical-device validation gates are still pending, so it is intentionally not linked as a live surface here. See the for its recorded state.

Created by Ian Winfield Dansereau.

Trust & provenance
  • Static, installable web app. It stores no cookies and uses no localStorage, sessionStorage, or analytics — all state is in-memory.
  • It makes no external network calls; everything shown is served from this site.
  • Reports and datasets marked Sample are synthetic demonstrations. They are illustrative and are not real incidents, findings, or production data.
  • System-map, deployment, and execution-gate states are hand-recorded from the Odisena master registry as of the 2026-07-13 cutoff — they are illustrative and are never polled, reconciled, or actioned from this console.
  • Content is shared as an engineering portfolio and is provided as-is, without warranties or guarantees.
